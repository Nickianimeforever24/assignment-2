Leonard Nimoy

as Spock

1931 - 2015

Leonard Nimoy was an actor, author director, singer and photographer. His most well know role was that of Spock in the Start Trek franchise. The character is a half-vulcan half-human star fleet officer. Together with James Kirk and the crew of the USS Enterprise, thay eplore the far reaches of space. Leonard portrayed Spock from 1964, in Star Trek: The Original Series to 2013 in the recent reboot film, Star Trek Into Darkness.
